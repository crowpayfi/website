  • • Follows you forever - there's no delete button

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about decentralized payments

What is Crowpay?
Crowpay is a P2P platform that lets you pay for anything in the real world using only cryptocurrency (USDC), with help from community members — no banks, exchanges, or credit cards required.
How does it work?
1. You describe what you need to pay
2. Another user accepts and makes the payment
3. Your USDC stays secure in smart contract
4. The payer receives crypto as reward
Is it safe?
Absolutely. We use smart contracts (escrow) on Solana to ensure payment is only released when confirmed. If something goes wrong, funds are automatically returned.
Do I need KYC?
No. Crowpay is 100% decentralized. You don't need to provide personal information, complete KYC, or connect bank accounts. Your privacy is protected.
Where can I use it?
Anywhere in the world. Since payments are made by real people, we don't depend on banks or country-specific payment methods.
Can I earn money with Crowpay?
Yes! If you have access to local payment methods (PIX, UPI, bank transfers, etc.), you can accept requests and earn USDC. Help others and get rewarded.
What if someone doesn't pay?
If the deadline expires without confirmation, USDC is automatically returned. We also have a reputation system to keep the community safe.
Which cryptocurrencies are supported?
Currently, only USDC on Solana network. More tokens may be supported in the future.
How do I become a Crowpayer?
Simple! Connect your wallet, enable "Receive requests" mode, and start helping others make payments using your local payment methods while earning USDC.
Why not use a crypto card?
Crypto cards still go through banks and can be tracked. With Crowpay, you never need to identify yourself — and nobody knows what you paid for or to whom. True financial privacy.
