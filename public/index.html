Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about peer-to-peer payments

What is Crowpay?
Crowpay is a P2P platform that lets you pay for anything in the real world using digital assets (USDC), with help from community members â€” direct peer-to-peer transactions.
How does it work?
1. You describe what you need to pay
2. Another user accepts and makes the payment
3. Your digital assets stay secure in smart contract
4. The payer receives digital tokens as reward
Is it safe?
Absolutely. We use smart contracts (escrow) on Solana to ensure payment is only released when confirmed. If something goes wrong, funds are automatically returned.
What verification is required?
Minimal. Crowpay uses blockchain technology. You interact directly with smart contracts using your digital wallet. Streamlined user experience.
Where can I use it?
Anywhere in the world. Since payments are made by community members, we leverage local payment infrastructure globally.
Can I earn money with Crowpay?
Yes! If you have access to local payment methods (PIX, UPI, bank transfers, etc.), you can accept requests and earn digital tokens. Help others and get rewarded.
What if someone doesn't pay?
If the deadline expires without confirmation, USDC is automatically returned. We also have a reputation system to keep the community safe.
Which digital assets are supported?
Currently, only USDC on Solana blockchain. More digital assets may be supported in the future.
How do I become a Crowpayer?
Simple! Connect your digital wallet, enable "Receive requests" mode, and start helping others make payments using your local payment methods while earning digital tokens.
Why not use a digital asset card?
Digital asset cards still require traditional banking infrastructure and processing intermediaries. With Crowpay, you interact directly with blockchain technology for streamlined peer-to-peer transactions. Enhanced technological efficiency.
